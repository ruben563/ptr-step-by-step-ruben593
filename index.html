The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are the permenaent structures four feet off the ground. Each turt has canvas walls, a wooden floor and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurtrs furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove Your luxury camping experience incudes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing with light layers of clothing.It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and sense of adventure!
